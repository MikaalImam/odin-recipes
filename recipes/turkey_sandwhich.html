Turkey Sandwhich Recipe

Back to Home

Description

This turkey sandwich is made with flaky croissant bread, tender slices of turkey, your favorite cheese, and a delicious sauce for extra flavor. It's a simple yet satisfying meal perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Croissant bread
  • Turkey slices
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Ketchap, Mayonnaise, Mustard and Hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the croissant horizontally; but do now cut it fully. Leave the top and bottom pices connected
  3. Layer the bottom half with turkey slices and cheddar cheese.
  4. Spread a mixture of ketchap, mayonnaise, mustard, and hot sauce on the top half of the croissant.
  5. Place the top half back on the sandwich.
  6. bake the croissant in the oven until the cheese has melted
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute.
  8. Cut the sandwich in half and serve warm.
